PROJECT DESCRIPTION2

The purpose of the project is to remove subsurface contamination related to releases from a former dry cleaner. Electrical resistance heating technology will be used to remediate the subsurface contamination. Cleanup will also facilitate future redevelopment of the Spic’N Span property.

BACKGROUND AND PROPERTY HISTORY

Years of dry cleaner operation on the property have resulted in releases of contaminants including PCE (“perc”) and mineral spirits into the soil and groundwater beneath the building. The contaminants have migrated beneath the neighboring property to the north, which is currently a parking lot owned by KeyBank and, also to the south east into the Maynard Avenue South right-of-way.
